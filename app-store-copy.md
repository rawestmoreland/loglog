## Description

LogLog is the social bathroom tracker that turns your daily routine into something worth logging.

Drop a pin every time nature calls, rate the restrooms you visit, track your gut health with the Bristol Stool Scale, and follow your friends' sessions in real time — all on a live map.

**WHAT LOGLOG DOES**

**Poop Map**
Log your sessions and pin them on a map. View your own history, filter by friends, or explore public activity in your area. Clusters keep things tidy; tap to dive in.

**Toilet Ratings**
Rate the restrooms you visit so your friends (and strangers) know what to expect. Find the best thrones near you before it's too late.

**Bristol Stool Scale**
Log each session with a Bristol score (1–7) to track your digestive health over time. The built-in Bristol reference helps you understand what your body is telling you.

**Poop Pals**
Follow friends and see their sessions pop up on the map in real time. Get push notifications when a pal is mid-session. Because shared suffering is half the struggle.

**Messaging**
Chat with your poop pals. Share tips, commiserate, or just check in.

Whether you're managing digestive health, tracking a condition, or just here for the laughs — LogLog makes your bathroom routine worth talking about.

Download LogLog. Start logging.
